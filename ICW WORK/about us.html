Our Origin

Founded in 2008, our organisation was born from the belief that access to quality education is the most powerful tool for national development.
We recognized poor education, teacher's shortage and committed to a collaborative, sustainable solution.
We believe that if we work together, we can change the education quality in Uganda, east Africa and Africa at large, making it meet the global
expectations and tirning the lives of lots of children.


Vision

To see a Uganda where every student has the skills and knowledge necessary to succeed in global economy.

Mission

To empower Ugandan schools and students through innovative programs focused on teacher development, infrastructure improvement, and community.

Our values

Commitment to Community
Transparency
Sustainability
Local Ownership
